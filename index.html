Biography

Hi, I'm Zhenghao Liu, currently a Ph.D. candidate affiliated with Key Laboratory of Quantum Information, CAS. My research orientations include foundations of quantum theory, quantum simulation and quantum information. I am adept at experimental investigation of quantum physics based on photonic architecture, and simultaneously have a solid theoretical background.

Previously, I obtained my bachelor of science degree in University of Science and Technology of China (2017), and was an exchange student in University of Michigan (2016).
