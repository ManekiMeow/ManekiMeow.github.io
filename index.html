Biography

I am a postdoc at Technical University of Denmark. My research orientation includes foundations of quantum theory, quantum simulation and quantum computation.

I obtained my Ph.D. in quantum information (2022) and B.Sc. in physics (2017) at the University of Science and Technology of China. I was an exchange student in University of Michigan (2016).

My Curriculum Vitae is available in English and Chinese.
